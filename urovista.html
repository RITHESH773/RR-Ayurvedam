Uro Vista Ayurvedic Treatment

What is Uro Vista?

Uro Vista is a specialized Ayurvedic therapy designed to rejuvenate and strengthen the chest region, particularly the heart and lungs. This treatment involves creating a herbal dough well over the chest and filling it with warm medicated oils or ghee. The therapy deeply nourishes the tissues, enhances circulation, and alleviates respiratory and cardiovascular ailments.

How Uro Vista Works

  • A medicated dough ring is placed on the chest region.
  • Warm herbal oils or ghee are poured into the well, allowing deep absorption.
  • The patient remains in a relaxed state while the therapy works on the chest tissues.
  • After a specific duration, the oil is removed, and the chest is gently massaged with herbal oils.
