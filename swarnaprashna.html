Swarnaprashna - The Golden Immunity Booster

A time-tested Ayurvedic practice to boost immunity, memory, and overall well-being of children.

Benefits of Swarnaprashna

  • Boosts immunity and digestion
  • Improves overall physical and mental health
  • Helps in speech and cognitive development
  • Protects from seasonal infections

Advantages of Swarnaprashna

  • 100% natural and safe Ayurvedic remedy
  • No side effects when taken as per recommended dosage
  • Strengthens respiratory and digestive systems
  • Improves concentration and learning ability
  • Enhances skin health and overall vitality

How Does Swarnaprashna Work?

Swarnaprashna consists of purified gold (Swarnabhasma) mixed with honey, ghee, and herbal extracts. It is given on specific days, particularly on **Pushya Nakshatra**, to enhance its efficacy. This unique blend nourishes the brain, improves digestion, and builds strong immunity.
