Trapanam Ayurvedic Treatment

Trapanam, also known as Netra Basti, is a specialized Ayurvedic therapy focused on rejuvenating and nourishing the eyes. This unique treatment involves creating a dough ring around the eyes and filling it with warm medicated ghee or herbal oils, allowing deep penetration to cleanse and strengthen ocular tissues.

How Trapanam Works

  • A herbal dough ring is placed around the eyes.
  • Warm medicated ghee or herbal oil is poured into the ring.
  • The patient opens and closes their eyes intermittently to absorb the medicinal properties.
  • After a specific duration, the ghee or oil is removed, and the eyes are gently cleansed.

Advantages of Trapanam

  • Strengthens and nourishes the eyes, enhancing vision.
  • Reduces eye strain, dryness, and fatigue.
  • Helps in managing conditions like cataracts, glaucoma, and night blindness.
  • Improves clarity and reduces dark circles and puffiness.
  • Promotes relaxation and alleviates stress affecting eye health.

Who Can Benefit from Trapanam?

  • Individuals suffering from dry eyes, blurry vision, or excessive tearing.
  • People experiencing eye strain due to prolonged screen time.
  • Those with weak eyesight, eye pain, or sensitivity to light.
  • Anyone looking to maintain optimal eye health naturally.

Post-Treatment Care

  • Avoid bright lights and excessive screen exposure immediately after treatment.
  • Follow a balanced Ayurvedic diet rich in nutrients beneficial for eye health.
  • Practice recommended eye exercises and relaxation techniques.
  • Use prescribed Ayurvedic eye drops for additional care.

Trapanam is a time-tested Ayurvedic therapy that provides deep nourishment, detoxification, and rejuvenation for the eyes. Experience the healing power of Ayurveda and enhance your vision naturally!
