Siro Takradhara Ayurvedic Treatment

What is Siro Takradhara?

Siro Takradhara is a unique Ayurvedic therapy where medicated buttermilk is poured over the forehead in a rhythmic and continuous stream. This therapy is highly effective in calming the nervous system, relieving stress, and treating various neurological and psychological disorders.

How Siro Takradhara Works

  • Warm, medicated buttermilk is prepared with specific herbs.
  • The patient lies on a therapy table in a relaxed position.
  • The buttermilk is poured in a steady stream over the forehead.
  • The therapy lasts for a specific duration, allowing deep absorption and relaxation.
  • After the treatment, the patient rests, followed by a gentle head massage.
